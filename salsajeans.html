ABOUT THE PROJECT

Salsa Jeans is a portuguese fashion label launched in 1995 that has embraced global influences to created bright, energetic casul wear.

‘Salsa Jeans’ is a European clothing brand that was created in Portugal. It currently has stores over 35 countries around the world. Salsa Jeans’ mission is to inspire Men and Women around the world by creating products that fits the lifestyle of its ideal customers. They aim to give consumers a confident and sensual appearance.
