ABOUT THE PROJECT

Alnafisah Fragrance is a uniqe option that has created a brand concept consisting of an oriental background.

Alnafisah is a fragrance store located in Miami, Florida. It specializes in custom Arabic-Oriental perfumes. This includes products such as perfume cologne, body oils, body sprays and scented lotion. In the Middle East Arabic perfumes have been advertised using various mediums specifically through social media platforms including Instagram and Snapchat via video bloggers. They have also been advertised in stores using fragrances samples. As a new brand Alnafisah will be marketing its products to older Millennials within the US using a strategic social media campaign and product placements to bring awareness to the brand
